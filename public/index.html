Welcome to Bruinswipes!

We are a marketplace that allows UCLA students and faculty to buy and sell meal swipes! Our mission is to provide all UCLA Bruins the opportunity to eat at our top-ranked dining halls. Using BruinSwipes is simple...

1. Login. Don't have an account? Create one here
2. Search our market for meal swipes to purchase or sell
3. Connect with a verified Bruin and buy/sell your meal swipe
